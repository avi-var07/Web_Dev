About Khaana Bank Trust

Khaana Bank Trust is a dedicated non-profit organization founded with the vision of creating a hunger-free society. We believe that access to nutritious food is a fundamental human right, and we work tirelessly to ensure no one goes to bed hungry.

Our comprehensive approach includes food distribution, community gardens, nutrition education, and sustainable development programs that empower communities to become self-sufficient.

Learn More About Us

Our Mission & Vision

Driven by compassion and guided by our core values, we strive to create lasting change in communities.

Our Mission

To eliminate hunger and food insecurity in our communities by providing nutritious meals, promoting sustainable agriculture, and empowering individuals through education and skills development.

Our Vision

A world where every individual has access to nutritious food, where communities are self-sufficient, and where hunger is eliminated through collective action and sustainable solutions.
